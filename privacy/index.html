11. Gmail Data Access

KeepSlip offers an optional feature that connects to your Gmail account to automatically find and import receipt emails. If you choose to enable this feature, KeepSlip requests read-only access to your Gmail inbox (gmail.readonly scope) via Google OAuth.


KeepSlip uses this access solely to identify emails that contain purchase receipts and to extract structured data from them (merchant name, date, and total amount). Raw email content, email bodies, and non-receipt emails are never read, stored, or shared. Only the extracted receipt data is saved to your account. You can revoke Gmail access at any time through your Google account settings or within the KeepSlip app, and your imported receipt data will be retained unless you delete it.
